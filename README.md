# Fractalism

A framework for noticing how patterns repeat across inner life, relationships, systems, and consciousness.

## What is this

Fractalism is a developing body of work. It is not a finished doctrine, not a religion, and not a political theory. It is a way of seeing.

The framework began from a simple observation: much of what shapes a life is not random. It repeats. In thoughts, in habits, in relationships, in institutions, and in the systems around you.

The core practical elements are:

- The Void: the interval between noticing a pattern and the automatic reaction that follows
- Friction as signal: the distinction between resistance that signals misalignment and resistance that appears because something old is being interrupted
- Truth: what reduces inner division after contact with it, rather than temporarily relieving it
