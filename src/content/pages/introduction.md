## Working premise

Fractalism begins from the observation that truth is not merely asserted but patterned. What is coherent in one layer of reality tends to reappear, in transformed form, at other scales.

## Scope

This introduction will eventually clarify the basic intent of the framework, the kind of discernment it asks for, and the kinds of questions it is meant to hold open.

## First direction

The project does not begin with the promise of a closed system. It begins with orientation, with attention, and with a disciplined effort to see without surrendering language to inversion.
