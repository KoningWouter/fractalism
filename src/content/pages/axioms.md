## Draft axioms

1. What is true tends toward coherence across scales.
2. What is inverted can mimic order while eroding reality contact.
3. Attention is never neutral; it is formative.
4. Ethics and perception cannot be cleanly separated.

## Status

These axioms are placeholders for a more deliberate first canon. They are meant to establish tone and structure, not to freeze the framework prematurely.
