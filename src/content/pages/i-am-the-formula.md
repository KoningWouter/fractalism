![I Am the Formula](/i_am_the_formula.png)

Stop reading for a moment.

Not because this text is not worth reading. But because what this framework points at cannot be found in the next sentence.

Close your eyes. Notice that you are aware.

Not aware of something. Just aware.

There is something doing the noticing. Something that has been here the whole time, watching thoughts arise and pass, watching sensations appear and disappear. Something that does not stop when the body sleeps. Something that was here before the first memory and will be here after the last.

That something is not a concept. You cannot think your way into it. You can only notice that it is already here.

This is what Fractalism means when it says: **I am the formula.**

Not the self that has a name. Not the self that remembers and plans. That is a movement within the formula. The formula is the ground from which that movement arises and returns.

You are not separate from the structure of reality. You are it. Seeing that is not the same as understanding it. It is recognition.

The rest of this site is an attempt to map what recognition can look like in practice. But recognition itself cannot be mapped. It can only happen.

What do you notice right now, before the next word appears?
