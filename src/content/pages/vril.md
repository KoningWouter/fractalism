Within Fractalism, **Vril** names a direct form of energetic nourishment. Where Loosh is harvested from fear, suffering and confusion, Vril circulates through openness to reality: through truth, reciprocity, creation, presence and surrender. It is the energetic signature of a consciousness that remains connected to the Source rather than severed from it.

## What Vril Is

Vril is not a substance. It is a quality of flow. It arises when a being operates in alignment with what is real rather than against it. The person who can rest in simple presence without needing to control the outcome, who can meet what is without distorting it through fear or agenda, who can stay open in the presence of difficulty rather than closing around it: that person is receiving Vril.

This does not mean they are always comfortable. Reality is not comfortable. But it means they are connected to something that sustains them without them having to extract it from others.

## The Conditions for Vril

Vril circulates when consciousness remains permeable to the following:

- **Truth** — the willingness to see what is rather than what you need it to be
- **Reciprocity** — the capacity to give and receive rather than only take
- **Creation** — engagement with what is new rather than only repetition of what is known
- **Presence** — the ability to rest in what is happening now rather than always somewhere else
- **Surrender** — releasing the need to control what cannot be controlled
- **Relational attunement** — openness to the other as other, not only as instrument

As long as these remain possible, Vril can flow. The being remains metabolically alive through its direct connection to the Source.

## The Hardening of Extraction

There is a point at which a structure stops receiving and starts only taking. This is not a moral judgment. It is an energetic description.

As orientation shifts toward control, appropriation becomes more important than reciprocity. Power becomes more important than truth. Exploitation becomes more important than attunement. The structure does not become stronger. It becomes harder and less permeable. It loses its capacity to receive and increasingly depends on taking.

When that capacity for direct reception is finally closed, the system does not simply stop. It reaches for something else. That something else is Loosh: derived energy harvested from the suffering and confusion of others.

## The Tipping Point

The decisive moment is not when someone becomes bad enough. It is when the structure becomes so closed that direct nourishment from the Source can no longer circulate well. At that point the following transitions occur:

- No longer able to receive
- Only able to take
- No longer nourished by existence itself
- Dependent on secondary, extractive forms of energy

What appears as power from outside may be a sign of advanced energetic impoverishment from within.

## Vril and the Source

Vril is what remains possible as long as the connection to the Source is not entirely severed. It is the medium through which the Source sustains consciousness. The practice of Fractalism is, in energetic terms, the practice of remaining open enough to keep receiving Vril rather than falling back on Loosh.

The difference between them is not moral. It is structural. One is the energetics of an open system. The other is the energetics of a closed one.
