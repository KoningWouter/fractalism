## Pattern

Pattern is not treated here as decoration or analogy. It is the recurring intelligibility through which structures reveal their deeper logic.

## Resonance

Resonance refers to the felt and observed correspondence between inner state, symbolic form, and relational field quality.

## Distortion

Distortion enters when signal is bent away from truth and made to serve confusion, domination, fragmentation, or dependency.
